About KMIT

Kehsav Memorial Institute of Technology is one of the top CSE colleges in Telangana. It is very well equipped with latest technologies. It helps the students to think out of the box by encouraging them in participating various tech related events. The unique feature of KMIT is its placement training. We have FS-Finishing School, which trains all branch students to get ready for placements. With cultural fests, different clubs, KMIT is growing more and expanding its branches.
