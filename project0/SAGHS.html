My School Journey

About SAGHS

St Anns Girls High School is a missionary school. It has celebrated its golden jubilee anniversary in the year 2015. It has classes from kindergarten to X standard. It has a ground with basketball court. It has a library, auditorium, playground and a small chapel. Every year there will be activities such as sports day, annual day, picnics and excursions. It has adapted to smart teaching with the use of projectors.

Life at SAGHS

My whole school life has been with this school. I have made life-long friends here. We have been together for 13 years. We had so many precious experieneces and memories that can never be forgotten. This picture is from the golden jubilee event that we all met for in 2015. I have participated in countless events and made best memories here. It is almost like a second home for me and I am proud to be an Annite.
